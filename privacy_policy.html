Privacy Policy for AnimOrg

Last Updated: June 20, 2026

Welcome to AnimOrg! This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the AnimOrg mobile application ("the App"). The App is developed and maintained in India. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe online experience.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with our anime tracking and community features, we collect the following types of information:

a. Personal Information (Provided by You)

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we may collect your email address, name, and profile picture.
  • Authentication Data: If you choose to sign in using Google Sign-In, we will receive your basic profile information (like your email address and name) from Google.
  • User-Generated Content: We collect the content you create and share, including community posts, comments, reviews, watchlists, profile information, and anime tracking history.

b. Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Information about your activity within the App, such as pages visited, features used, and interactions with other users.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and crash reports.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the App's features (e.g., saving your watchlists and anime tracking progress).
  • To manage your user account and verify your identity through Firebase Authentication.
  • To enable community interactions, allowing you to share posts, leave comments, and connect with other users.
  • To analyze app usage and diagnose technical issues or crashes to improve stability.
  • To communicate with you regarding account updates, security alerts, or support messages.

3. Third-Party Services We Use

We use secure, trusted third-party services to power the App. These services may collect and process information as described in their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase & Firestore: We use Firebase for backend infrastructure, database storage, and crash reporting. For more information, please refer to the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Sign-In: Used for secure authentication. You can manage your Google account permissions directly from your Google account settings.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. We may disclose your data only in the following scenarios:

  • Public Community Information: Your profile picture, username, community posts, comments, and public watchlists are visible to other users of the App.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

5. Data Retention and Deletion (Google Play Data Safety)

We retain your personal information only for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you the services.

How to Delete Your Data:

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data at any time. You can do this in two ways:

  • In-App Deletion: Navigate to the Settings menu within the AnimOrg app, select "Account," and choose "Delete Account." This action is irreversible and will delete your authentication records, profile, watchlists, and community comments/posts.
  • Email Request: If you no longer have access to the app, you can request data deletion by contacting us at our developer email (provided on the Google Play Store listing or below). Please email us from the email address associated with your account.

6. Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices (such as those provided by Firebase Auth) to safeguard your data. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
